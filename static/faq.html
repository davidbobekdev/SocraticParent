Everything you need to know about Socratic Parent

What is Socratic Parent?

+

Socratic Parent is an AI-powered homework helper that teaches you how to coach your child using the Socratic method. Instead of giving answers, we give you the right questions to ask.

Snap a photo of any homework problem, and get a personalized coaching script with questions that guide your child to discover the solution themselves.

What subjects are supported?

+

We support all major subjects:

Math: Arithmetic, algebra, geometry, calculus

From kindergarten through high school!

How is this different from other homework apps?

+

Traditional homework solvers just give the answer — your child copies it and learns nothing.

Socratic Parent gives you a coaching script with questions that help your child think through the problem. They learn the concept, not just the answer.

It's like having a master teacher guide you through homework time.

Do you store my child's homework images?

+

No. Privacy is our top priority.

Images are processed in real-time and immediately discarded. We never store your homework photos on our servers.
