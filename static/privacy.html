Privacy Policy

Last Updated: January 25, 2026

Quick Summary: We process homework images to provide educational guidance but do NOT permanently store them. Your uploaded images are deleted immediately after analysis.

1. Information We Collect

Account Information

When you register, we collect:

  • Email address (for account identification and communication)
  • Password (securely hashed using bcrypt)
  • Registration date and last login time

Usage Data

We automatically collect:

  • Number of homework analyses performed
  • Premium subscription status
  • API usage patterns (for rate limiting)

Homework Images

When you upload homework images:

  • Images are sent to Google Gemini AI for analysis
  • Images are processed in memory only
  • Images are NOT saved to our servers
  • Images are deleted immediately after processing

2. How We Use Your Information

We use collected information to:

  • Provide the Service and generate educational guidance
  • Authenticate your account and maintain security
  • Process subscription payments through Paddle
  • Track usage limits (3 free scans per day)
  • Communicate important service updates
  • Improve the Service based on usage patterns

3. Data Sharing and Third Parties

We share minimal data with trusted third parties:

Google Gemini AI

  • Homework images are sent to Google's Gemini API for processing
  • Google processes images according to their Privacy Policy
  • We use Google's API for educational purposes only

Paddle (Payment Processor)

  • Payment information is handled entirely by Paddle
  • We receive subscription status updates via webhooks
  • We do NOT store credit card information
  • Paddle's Privacy Policy applies to payment data

Railway (Hosting Provider)

  • The Service is hosted on Railway's cloud infrastructure
  • Railway may log access for security and performance monitoring

4. Data Storage and Security

We implement security measures to protect your data:

  • Passwords: Hashed using bcrypt (never stored in plain text)
  • Authentication: JWT tokens with secure HTTP headers
  • Data Persistence: Account data stored in encrypted JSON format
  • Image Storage: No permanent storage of uploaded images
  • HTTPS: All communications encrypted in transit

5. Data Retention

  • Account Data: Retained as long as your account is active
  • Homework Images: Deleted immediately after analysis (not retained)
  • Usage Logs: Maintained for service operation and debugging

6. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access: Request a copy of your personal data
  • Correction: Update or correct your account information
  • Deletion: Request account deletion by contacting support
  • Export: Request export of your account data
  • Opt-Out: Unsubscribe from marketing emails (if any)

7. Children's Privacy

The Service is designed for parents and guardians (18+ years). While parents may use the Service to help children with homework, we do not knowingly collect personal information directly from children under 13.

8. Cookies and Tracking

We use minimal tracking technologies:

  • JWT Tokens: Stored in browser localStorage for authentication
  • Session Data: Maintained during active use
  • No Third-Party Cookies: We do not use advertising or tracking cookies

9. International Data Transfers

Your data may be transferred to and processed in countries where our service providers operate (primarily United States). By using the Service, you consent to such transfers.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify users of material changes by:

  • Updating the "Last Updated" date
  • Sending email notifications for significant changes
  • Displaying notices within the Service
